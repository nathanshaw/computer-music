The Theia board is an Arduino Uno shield that supports up to
8 SR-04 Ultrasonic Rangefinders.

Theia is a greek goddess and is directly translated from greek to mean “goddess” or 
“divine”. She is the mother of Helios, Selene and Eos the gods of the sun, the moon and the dawn respectively. She is the eldest daughter of Gaia and Uranus the gods of the Earth and Sky, giving her a primal origin.
